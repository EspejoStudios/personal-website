Profile

A little about me.

IE

Hi. I'm Ivyan Espejo — Lead UX/UI Designer, Front-End Developer, and the kind of person who has been building things on screens since 1980. Yes, 1980. I've been getting paid handsomely for it since 1998.

I'm a roll-up-my-sleeves, let's-solve-the-actual-problem kind of designer. I love the full arc of the creative process — from the first rough sketch on a whiteboard to the last pixel of a polished interface. "It works" is the bare minimum. I'm here to make it exceptional.

Currently Lead UX/UI Designer & Developer at BCBS Global Solutions, where I lead a team of designers, serve as the company's WCAG and Analytics SME, and ship across web, mobile, and enterprise platforms. I also run Espejo Studios — my independent design and development practice — where I work with clients on everything from brand identity to full-stack app builds.

I believe great design is invisible, accessibility is not optional, and the best products are built by people who genuinely give a damn. Let's build something.
