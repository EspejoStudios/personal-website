About

Clarity is the
real innovation.

Espejo Studios is a design and engineering studio building products that real people depend on — from healthcare platforms processing millions of claims to AI systems that run on a Raspberry Pi in your hallway.

Our work sits where human intuition meets machine logic. We don't believe in design that only looks good in a pitch deck. We believe in design that survives contact with the real world.

Deep roots in music tech, NYU-trained, and a conviction that "it works" is the floor, not the ceiling.
